Product Name: Shukuma
Founder: Yanga Ngcayisa

Objective:
Shukuma is designed to provide users with a simple, engaging, and convenient way to stay active without the hassle of a gym or complex workout routines. Through a randomized card-based system, the app ensures variety, excitement, and accessibility to fitness. The app aims to replicate the innovative approach of the physical exercise card pack in a digital format, allowing users to access workouts anytime, anywhere.

Key Features:
1. Randomised Workouts:
Users can shuffle the deck and pick a random workout card each day.

Offers a surprise workout every time, keeping users engaged and motivated.

2. Clear Instructions & Visuals:
Each workout card contains easy-to-follow, step-by-step instructions with accompanying visuals.

Ensures users know exactly how to perform each exercise correctly and safely.

3. Fitness Challenges & Goals:
Daily and weekly challenges to encourage consistency and help users track progress.

Users can set goals, track performance, and receive rewards or achievements to maintain motivation.

4. No Equipment Necessary:
All exercises can be done with body weight, requiring no special equipment.

Perfect for home, travel, or outdoor workouts.

5. Customisable Workouts:
Users can filter workouts based on fitness level, workout duration, or specific goals (e.g., cardio, strength, flexibility).

Ability to tailor workouts to suit individual needs.

6. Progress Tracker:
Built-in progress tracker logs each workout completed, tracks streaks, and monitors improvement.

Users can see workout history, progress over time, and set new goals.

7. Portable & Convenient:
Replicates the convenience of the physical card pack, offering easy access to workouts anytime, anywhere.

Available on both Android and iOS.

8. Community & Social Integration:
Social features allow users to share progress, achievements, and challenge friends.

Integration with social media platforms and a built-in app community enhances engagement and accountability.

New Additional Features:
9. Tasks:
Daily/Quick access to assigned or set tasks for better workout organization.

10. Self-Regulation Tools:
Journaling feature to track thoughts and reflections on fitness and wellness.

Breathwork exercises for relaxation and focus.

White noise for concentration and stress management.

Trigger management: Users can identify, manage, and respond to stress triggers.

11. Connection & Social Features:
Users can assign workout cards to friends or tag a friend to join them.

Supports and celebrates friends moving through the card workouts.

12. Challenges:
Daily Card Challenge

Streak Challenge

Most Cards Done Challenge

Time-Based Challenge

Group Challenge for team-based motivation

13. Nutrition Support:
Food Journal for tracking daily meals and nutrition habits.

Meal Guides providing healthy meal plans and recipes.

Technology Stack:
Front-End:
Native mobile development (React Native, Flutter, or native Swift for iOS and Kotlin for Android).

Back-End:
Cloud-based infrastructure (Firebase, Node.js with Express) for user authentication, data storage, and notifications.

Database:
Firebase or MongoDB for storing user data (workout history, progress, settings).

Analytics & Tracking:
Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics for tracking user interactions, performance, and app growth.

Push Notifications:
Firebase Cloud Messaging or OneSignal for sending workout reminders, motivational messages, or new challenge alerts.

 Strategy:
Freemium Model:
Free version with access to a limited number of workouts and basic tracking features.

Premium version offering more workouts, advanced tracking, fitness goals, and exclusive challenges.

In-App Purchases:
Users can buy additional workout packs or features, such as personalised fitness plans or special challenges.

Subscription Model:
Monthly or yearly subscription for access to the full range of workouts and premium features.
